# PRODUCT.md — LineHaul Station

## Register
**Brand / marketing** (design IS the product). Multi-audience marketing site; a visitor's impression is the deliverable. Long-scroll, art-directed, conversion-oriented.

## What this is
The marketing site for **LineHaul Station** — a national, shared-use truck-terminal network that runs a freight **relay** ("Modern-Day Pony Express"): trailers hand off Hub-to-Hub so freight moves 24/7 and drivers get home daily. Next.js 14 (App Router) · React 18 · TypeScript · Tailwind. The first Hub is open in West Memphis, AR (I-40 / I-55).

## Who it's for
One master brand hub (`/`) routing to five audience lanes, each with its own accent and program:
- **Drivers** (`#F07820`) — OneHome + the Outriders Club. Free membership; the emotional, club-forward lane. *Primary conversion: Join Free.*
- **Carriers** (`#4878A8`) — FlexSpace terminal access; per-tier cost calculator.
- **Brokers** (`#7EC8E3`) — reliable relay capacity & positioning.
- **Shippers** (`#18A848`) — in-route secure staging, just-in-time arrival.
- **Government** (`#C8A060`) — macro freight-relay infrastructure (safer roads, better jobs, decarbonization).
- Plus **/leadership** (founder + board) and **/join** (Outriders membership).
Audiences skew non-technical and field-based (drivers especially). Trust, dignity, and concrete numbers matter more than feature lists.

## Purpose
Communicate the network and convert the right action per lane — **Join Free** (drivers/Outriders), **Connect With Us / Schedule a Call** (carriers/brokers/shippers/government). Make a freight-industry audience feel respected, not sold to.

## Brand personality
Rugged · premium · engineered · human. The system voice: a freight control-room / wayfinding aesthetic — **Carbon dominates, Chrome/Steel structures, Fuel Orange ignites (sparingly)**. Driver-respecting copy ("Knights of the Highway", "treat the industry's best drivers with dignity and respect"). Confident, plain-spoken, never corporate-glib.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS-cream landing pages and the hero-metric template.
- Truck-stop / highway-clip-art clichés.
- Corporate fleet-blue-and-gold logistics-vendor look.
- AI-grammar scaffolding: an eyebrow above every section, identical card grids, gradient text.

## Strategic design principles
1. **Per-audience accent is the signal.** Chrome/steel + carbon/white carry structure; the lane's accent drives kickers, coins, rules, CTAs only.
2. **Alternating black ↔ white rhythm.** No two adjacent sections share a background; `image`/`gradient`/`blueprint` are deliberate dark accents.
3. **Brand vocabulary is non-negotiable.** Hubs · Private Terminals · Service Centers — never "nodes". Exact casing: LineHaul Station · FlexSpace · OneHome · Outriders Club.
4. **CTA golden rule.** Only "Connect With Us" / "Schedule a Call" / "Join Free". Never Sign Up / Register / Submit.
5. **Legibility first.** White text over photos always sits on a darkened overlay; verify AA.
6. **Chrome/steel is a premium accent, not wallpaper.** Frames, coins, pills, rules, one glint moment — used judiciously.
7. **Reduced-motion safe & responsive.** Every animation has a reduced-motion path; clean at 390 / 768 / 1440.
